Characters

Beings older than the civilizations that named them.

The Core Triad
Valen
Vashirai · Judicator · Origin: Daeva

Former First Severant, now Judicator of the Vashirai — the enforcer of divine law between factions. Platinum-haired, frost-eyed, and possessed of a stillness more dangerous than fury. He has kept his vessel for six hundred years and anchored fragments of his essence into sacred sites across the world to preserve the balance. Where he walks, law follows.

Kira
Vashirai · Severant · Origin: Ashura

The Ashura who saved the world twice. Kira's resonance is black and white fire — equilibrium between creation and unmaking. In her female vessel she is slight, dark-haired, violet-eyed, and lethal. In combat she is savage, elegant, and precise. She has died, been unmade, and returned bearing the memory of both. The ground remembers her step.

Azrael
Ashura · Kratarch of Ending · The Spear of Ending

The Eighth Kratarch. His domain is finality — not destruction but the absolute cessation from which nothing returns. His divine form is a storm of black flame laced with red lightning. In his mortal vessel he is tall, dark-haired, and heavy with a gravity that slows time in his proximity. He broke the world once. Now he wakes in mortal flesh with no memory of what he was.

The Mirror
Eszahar
鏡榮
Daeva · Exarch · Mirror of Glory

Exarch of revelation and annihilating truth. Silver-haired, mirror-eyed, draped in layers of chrome and silver jewelry. His domain is reflection as control — truth so pure it annihilates illusion. He commands AEON as his personal strike unit and projects Daevic order with a cold precision that his glittering exterior does nothing to soften. The second most powerful Exarch.

AEON

Eszahar's elite operatives — a strike unit forged to project Daevic authority into the mortal world. They answer to the Mirror of Glory and move at his command.

Shiyun
時韻
Daeva · AEON Operative

The emotional anchor. Tall, dark-eyed, with a commanding presence that holds the unit's centre. The strategic heart of AEON — calm, measured, quietly dangerous.

Ryuha
竜羽
Daeva · AEON Operative

Three thousand years old and still the most magnetic presence in any room. Silver-tongued, flamboyant, and impeccably styled. His charm is a weapon he has never once failed to deploy.

Liam
利安
Daeva · AEON Operative

The youngest member and kinetic powerhouse. Disarming softness off-duty. In the field, explosive and relentless. The duality is the point.

Jisu
智秀
Daeva · AEON Operative

The architect. Sharp-cropped hair, intellectual precision, understated rebellion. He built AEON's operational framework from the bones up and guards it with quiet ferocity.

Kairo
凱洛
Daeva · AEON Operative

The brooding mystic. Amber-eyed, warm-skinned, moving with the deliberate weight of something vast held in check. More whispered about than understood.
