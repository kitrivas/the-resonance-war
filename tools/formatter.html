Story Formatter

Paste from Word/Docs or plain text. Italics from rich text are preserved automatically.

Markup you add before pasting:
## Your Heading Here → becomes <h2>
--- on its own line → becomes a section break
*** on its own line → also a section break
*italic text* → becomes <em> (only needed for plain text; Word italics auto-detected)
Tip: Add ## before headings in your source text. Example: ## I. The Dordogne — December 1943
