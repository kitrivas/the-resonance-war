Story Formatter

Paste your story from Word, Docs, or plain text. The formatter will:
• Wrap each paragraph in <p> tags
• Convert -- and — to &mdash;
• Convert "quotes" to smart quotes
• Preserve italics pasted from Word/Docs (or use *text* for manual italics)
• Lines starting with ## become <h2> headings
• Blank lines with *** or --- or === become section breaks
